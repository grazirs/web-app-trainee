Trainee Program 2022

About

The Codeminer42 Trainee Program is a 3-month continuing education project for those who have a bachelor degree or are finishing their graduation this year.

Our goal is to prepare the participants to act professionally as a developer on our projects. Participants will have access to professionals with experience in the market and a daily schedule of tasks and challenges.

You will learn to learn by solving real problems, training your research skills, collaborating with your peers, and getting down to business.

This is a program created by Codeminer42, a 10-year-old company specializing in software development for clients in Brazil and the United States.
